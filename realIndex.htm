<h2> ABOUT WATCH_DOGS WeareData </h2>
<div class="text-cols">
<div class="text-col">
<p>In the video game <a href="http://www.watchdogs.com">Watch_Dogs</a>, the <strong>city of Chicago</strong> is run by a <strong>Central Operating System (CTOS)</strong>. This system use <strong>data</strong> to manage the entire city and to solve complex problems. Traffic jams, war against crime, power management...</p>
<p>This is not fiction anymore. Smart cities are real, it’s happening now. A huge amount of data is collected and managed every day in our modern cities, and those data are available for everybody.</p>
<p><strong>Watch_Dogs WeareData</strong> is the first <strong>website to gather publicly available data about Paris, London and Berlin</strong>, in one location. Each of the three towns is recreated on a 3D map, allowing the user to discover the data that organizes and runs modern cities today, in real time. It also displays information about the inhabitants of these cities, via their social media activity.</p>
</div>
<div class="text-col">
<p><strong class="grey_upp">What you will discover here are only facts and reality.</strong></p>
<p><span class="whitecopy">Watch_Dogs WeareData gathers available <strong>geolocated data</strong> in a non-exhaustive way: we only display the information for which we have been given the authorization by the sources. Yet, it is already a huge amount of data. You may even watch what other users are looking at on the website through Facebook connect.</span></p>
</div>
</div>
